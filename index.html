03 // TIMELINE DEGRADATION

THE EVOLUTION OF RELIANCE

STAGE I : AUGMENTATION (2022)

The Calculator Phase

AI is utilized strictly for syntax correction, grammar checking, and basic structural outlines. The human remains the primary architect of the core idea.

STAGE II : CO-CREATION (2024)

The Synthesis Phase

Students begin prompting AI to generate paragraphs and core arguments. The human role shifts from 'Creator' to 'Editor', merely refining the machine's output.

STAGE III : DEPENDENCE (2026)

The Automation Phase

Prompting replaces thinking. Entire methodologies, codebases, and essays are generated zero-shot. The human role is reduced to merely 'Approving' the output.
